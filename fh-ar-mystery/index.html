FH AR Mystery

A student has disappeared at FH St. Pölten.
Follow the clues, scan the markers, and uncover the truth.

How to play

  1. Tap Start Camera and scan the image marker.
  2. Read the AR clue that appears.
  3. When finished, tap “Go to next clue →”
    (This is required to continue.)
  4. Go to the next location described in the clue.
  5. If you're stuck, tap “Need a hint?”.
